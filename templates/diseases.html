1. Leaf Blight

Caused by fungi that infect leaves, resulting in brown or black spots. Prevent by crop rotation and fungicides.

2. Powdery Mildew

White powdery spots on leaves due to fungal growth. Avoid by improving air circulation.

3. Rust

Orange or brown pustules appear on leaves. Common in humid environments.
